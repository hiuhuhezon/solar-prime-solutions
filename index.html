Who We Are

Founded in 2019, Solar Prime Solutions was inspired by the desire to provide high-quality solar products at affordable rates, helping clients reduce their electricity bills and avoid power outages.

We work with homeowners and businesses to offer tailor-made solar solutions for lighting, powering, pumping, and water heating needs. Our expertise extends to collaborating with various brands for inverters, batteries, and solar panels.

We also provide comprehensive consulting services that include system design, product recommendations, and professional installation to ensure optimal performance and maximum savings for our clients.
